About

Combined Electro-Mechanical Supply Inc. (the "Company") was incorporated in the Philippiines on August 16, 2000 with SEC reg. A200012644. The Primary purpose is to engage in the business of trading of goods such as electrical equipment and components, electrical products and spare parts. The secondary purpose is to engage in the business of electrical works, services and maintenance.

Mission

CEMS Inc. is a Sales, Marketing and Contracting Company established to service the Electrical, Automation and Mechanical requirements of Industrial and Commercial establists.

CEMS Inc. is dedicate to provide products and services of high quality in terms of loose product distribution, package engineered systems, project management, project implementations and after sales services. CEMS Inc. is your ally in your quest for excellence.
